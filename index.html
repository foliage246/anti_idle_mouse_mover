為什麼我看不到滑鼠移動？

Why can't I see the mouse move?

這是刻意設計的！為了不干擾您的正常使用，程式只會將滑鼠移動一個像素然後立刻移回。這個移動小到肉眼無法察覺，但足以讓系統偵測到活動，以防止電腦進入閒置狀態。

This is by design! To avoid interrupting your work, the app only moves the mouse by one pixel and immediately moves it back. This movement is too small to be seen but is enough for the system to detect activity and prevent it from going idle.
